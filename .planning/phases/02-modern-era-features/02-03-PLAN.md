<objective>
Restrict game outcome model training to the modern NBA era (2013-14+) and exclude the 2019-20 bubble and 2020-21 shortened seasons, then validate that modern-era accuracy meets or exceeds full-history accuracy.

Purpose: The post-2014 NBA plays fundamentally differently (pace, three-point emphasis, position-less basketball). Training on mixed-era data introduces structural non-stationarity that hurts prediction accuracy. The bubble and shortened seasons had anomalous conditions (neutral-site games, compressed schedule) that add noise rather than signal.

Output: Updated `game_outcome_model.py` with `MODERN_ERA_ONLY = True`, `MODERN_ERA_START = "201314"`, `EXCLUDED_SEASONS`, and a retrained model artifact.
</objective>
