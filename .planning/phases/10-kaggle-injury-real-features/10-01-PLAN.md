    - Output schema: player_id (int), player_name (str), team_id (int), game_id (str),
      game_date (YYYY-MM-DD), season (int), min_roll5 (float, 2dp), usg_pct (float, 4dp), was_absent (int)
    - Saves to data/raw/injuries/player_absences.csv (creates dir if needed)
    - Returns the DataFrame
