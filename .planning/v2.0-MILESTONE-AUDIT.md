## Critical Gaps

### FEAT-01 (UNSATISFIED)
`player_absences.csv` produced by Phase 10 is never imported by `injury_proxy.py` or `team_game_features.py`. The proxy still self-derives absence independently. The Phase 10 deliverable is orphaned.  
**Fix:** Modify `injury_proxy.py` to load `player_absences.csv` as its source instead of re-deriving.

### MODEL-03 (UNSATISFIED)
`get_strong_value_bets()` calls `run_value_bet_scan()` and filters by `edge_magnitude > 0.08`. `ats_model.pkl` is never loaded. No composite game-outcome + ATS signal exists.  
**Fix:** Load `ats_model.pkl`, run `predict_ats()`, combine with edge signal to produce ranked composite bets.
